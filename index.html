How to Use DAOOnboardingSwap on BSC

This contract lets users join the DAO by sending BNB based on a live USD price. In return, they receive 7,454,385,000 NWR5 tokens. The contract uses Chainlink to convert USD into BNB dynamically.

  1. Connect MetaMask to BNB Chain.

  3. Call calculateRequiredBNB(usdPrice) to compute the exact BNB to send.
  4. Send a transaction to swap() with the BNB amount.
